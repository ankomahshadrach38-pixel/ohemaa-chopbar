About Us

Ohemaa Chopbar — Where Tradition Meets Taste

We are a proudly Ghanaian chopbar located at the heart of Ofankor Barrier. Our mission is simple: serve the most authentic, flavourful Ghanaian meals made from fresh ingredients every single day.

Whether you’re craving a steaming bowl of fufu with light soup, banku and tilapia, or a hearty plate of amotuo — Ohemaa Chopbar is your home away from home.
